Seth Medders

Software engineer, graphic designer, musician, writer, and videographer.

Biography

Matiorasu graduated from Jacksonville State University in Jacksonville, Alabama in December of 2021, claiming a bachelor's degree in computer information systems. Since then, he has worked both freelance jobs and 9-5 retail. Matiorasu has earned recognitions in his area such as performing and coordinating with the All-County Honor Band of Calhoun County. Additionally, he achieved performance honors with the Alabama Music Educator's Association for his work with the Weaver High School Marching Band. In his studies, he achieved Jacksonville State University's Dean's List multiple times and was accepted into the school's prestigious Honors Society.

Professional Experience

Matiorasu has drawn experience from a variety of diverse fields in his professional life.

From August of 2016 to March of 2017, Matiorasu worked as a guest choreographer for the Weaver High School marching band, where he developed choreography for students, refined performances to specifications provided by the band's director by working one-on-one with performers, and prepared student groups for competition with routines, exercise regimens, and instructions on technique.

In the months of June and July 2017, Matiorasu interned with Widenet Consulting, LLC in Anniston, Alabama. In that time, he provided his programming knowledge and excellent customer service. In coordinating with his clients and coworkers, he provided a unique and robust product and ensured client satisfaction. Utilizing expertise and team skills, he boosted productivity and cohesion within the company.

After taking a break from contract work to pursue freelance art and music as well as focus on his studies, Matiorasu went to work for Wal-Mart in Jacksonville, Alabama. There, he provided excellent customer service and organizational expertise, as well as helped to organize schedules and reduce strain on his coworkers.
