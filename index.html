The Virtual Piano

The piano you see above was written with a blend of javascript, CSS, and html. You can play it with your mouse, or you can play it with your bottom row of letter keys! Z, X, C, V, B, N and M play the white keys, and S, D, G, H and J play the black keys.

Other Projects

Heat Index Calculator

The Heat Index Calculator, written in java, does just what you would expect-- It takes inputs for heat and humidity and calculates the predicted heat index.

Phone Number Registry

The Phone Number Registry, written in java, allows a user to create a registry of up to 30 profiles of names and associated phone numbers. Once created, the profiles can be searched by name to find the associated phone number.
